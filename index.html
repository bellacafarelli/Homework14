Drawing Machine

Description

This is a Five Nights at Freddy's themed drawing machine! Click the "Play/Pause" button to listen to Music. The buttons with the animatronic's names will make images of them appear in random places. Click the "Surprise" button multiple times for fun images! Click on the screen to place coins. If you don't like your work, you can clear the canvas. If you love it, you can save it to your device!
